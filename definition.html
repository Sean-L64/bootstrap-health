What is media?

Media is a form of communication that can come physically like a newspaper or most commonly digitally usually on the internet. Some examples include newspapers, video games, TV programs, social media, books, and blogs. Digital media is considred one of the more popular ways to consume media because looking at just the news side, about 8 in 10 Americans or 86% using a device to get the latest news, however 68% of Americans still use their TV to get the latest news.

Main issue

The problem is despite how mainstream media is, a lot could still contain unintended messages that spread quickly due to how common media is whether it's positive or negative and whether audiences are aware of the messsage or not. With the rise of social networking and digital media usage, the way we also connect through our devices has sparked another challenge that affects people's health physically & mentally.

Socially, consuming media during long durations doesn't involve much talking or interacting with other people around you or even though calling exists, it's not the same as talking to people in real life.

Mentally, being isolated and watching or experiencing potentially violent or inappropriate content for potentially hours on end damage brain development, lowers self-esteem or desensitizes emotions.

Physically, literally watching or playing games usually doesn't involve actual physical movement and there are some commercials out there that promote unhealthy products & lifestyles. This prevents physical exercise which benefits every part of the body physically and even mood-wise.
